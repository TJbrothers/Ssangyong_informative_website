Musso





Like most Ssangyongs, the overall exterior design is cleanly executed, portraying not only a sense of sportiness but also mixed with a stash of style. Open the door and you are immediately greeted with a clean and well-appointed cabin. Aside from its spaciousness, the Musso comes with generous amount of tech. Including its state-of-the-art infotainment system which includes an 8-inch HD screen, Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and Bluetooth connectivity and a Dual-zone fully automatic air-conditioner.
