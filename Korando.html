Korando





The Korando is a crossover that mixes aggressive body lines with a city-oriented design. It features a complete kit of LED headlights, tail light, fog lamps as well as daytime running lights. The front carries the same design language as its Tivoli stablemate but has a slightly different lower bumper. For the rear expect the same head-turning features as the front. A silver accent piece breaks and further defines the rear character lines of the crossover. it has an all-black interior with the centerpiece being the 10.25-inch supervision cluster display and the 8-inch infotainment system.
